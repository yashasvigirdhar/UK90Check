Privacy Policy for UK90Check

Last updated: March 2024

Overview

UK90Check is a Chrome extension designed to help users track their days spent outside the UK to ensure compliance with the UK's 90-day rule. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Storage

UK90Check operates entirely locally on your device:

  • All data (travel dates and start date) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • No data is transmitted to any external servers
  • No personal information is collected or processed

Permissions

The extension requires the following permission:

  • storage: Used only to save your travel dates and start date locally in your browser

No other permissions are requested or required.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is not accessible to other websites or extensions
  • Data is not shared with any third parties

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser data for the extension
  • Uninstalling the extension
